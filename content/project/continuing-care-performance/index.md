## Overview

As **Senior Performance Measurement Specialist** at the Saskatchewan Ministry of Health (Financial Services Branch), this ongoing initiative supports the Ministry's multi-year performance accountability framework across all major care and foundational areas.

## Key Activities

- **Continuing Care Performance Framework Development** — Led indicator mapping and development of accountability measures for continuing care services, aligning with SHA Performance Agreements and CIHI benchmarks
- **Multi-year Strategic Planning Support** — Providing analytical support across Better Results, Quality and Patient Experience, Primary Care, Continuing Care, Mental Health & Addictions (MHA), Human Health Resources (HHR), and Infrastructure pillars
- **Action-Outcome Performance Mapping** — Developed comprehensive action-outcome mappings across all care and foundational areas for multi-year key deliverables aligned with the Ministry Business Plan, Tactical Plan, and Strategic Plan
- **Dashboard & Visualization Appraisal** — Critically reviews analytical outputs, dashboards, and data visualizations to ensure accuracy and fitness for senior decision-makers
- **Stakeholder Collaboration** — Works with SHA, CIHI, and cross-branch stakeholders on performance agreement frameworks

## Impact

Directly supports evidence-based policy decisions affecting continuing care, acute care, and mental health services for the people of Saskatchewan, contributing to improvements in patient experience, safety, and system efficiency.
